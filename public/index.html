Frequently Asked Questions

Is this QR code generator really free?

Yes, QR Canvas is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or credit card requirements. You can create unlimited professional QR codes without ever signing up.

Will my custom QR code expire?

No. Our codes are static, meaning the data is encoded directly into the pixel pattern. They will never expire and will work as long as the content (like your URL) is active.

How do I make sure it scans?

We automatically use Level H (30%) Error Correction for all branded codes. To ensure perfect results, always test your code with your phone camera before printing at scale.

Can I use it for commercial purposes?

Absolutely! You can use these codes for business cards, billboards, menus, websites, and any marketing material you need.
